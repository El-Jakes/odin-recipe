Pegu Club Cocktail

One delicious gin cocktail, with Cointreau, lime juice, and bitters. It's practically a gin margarita.

By Lorem Ipsum

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 2 fluid ounce gin
  • ¾ fluid ounce orange-flavored liquer
  • ½ fluid ounce fresh lime juice
  • 2 dashes aromatic bitters
  • lime

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add gin, Cointreau, and lime juice. Shake in bitters. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a slice of lime.
